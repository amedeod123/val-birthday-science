Clinical Studies

Collagen Increase & Wrinkle Reduction

A controlled trial found that red and near-infrared light treatment significantly improved fine lines, wrinkles, and skin roughness while increasing intradermal collagen density compared to control groups.

Source: National Institutes of Health (PMC)

Reversing Visible Signs of Aging

A photobiomodulation study using red LEDs (630 nm) over 3 months confirmed that red light therapy can reverse visible signs of skin aging, with measurable improvements in texture, tone, and elasticity.

Source: PMC — Photobiomodulation Research

Skin Elasticity & Rejuvenation

A multi-center, randomized, double-blind, sham-controlled study found LED and infrared phototherapy to be effective, safe, well-tolerated, and painless for skin rejuvenation. Skin elasticity was significantly higher in the LED group at 6- and 8-week follow-ups.

Source: PubMed — Randomized Controlled Trial

How It Works at the Cellular Level

Red and near-infrared light is absorbed by mitochondria in skin cells, boosting cellular energy (ATP) production. This stimulates fibroblasts to produce more collagen and elastin — the proteins responsible for firm, youthful skin.

Source: Stanford Medicine
